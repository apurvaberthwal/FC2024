About Me


Ahoy there, fellow traveler! Don't mind the leaves swirling around me, it's just my grand entrance, you see! Call me Venti, a humble bard with a knack for windstorms and witty rhymes. My lyre, Windward Harp, is my trusty companion, whispering tales of ancient dragons and dandelion wishes.

Think of me like a dandelion seed, light and carefree, bouncing on the breeze from Mondstadt's grassy plains to Liyue's bustling harbors. I've witnessed sunrises over snow-capped mountains and sunsets over dandelion fields, collecting stories like seashells on a beach. So, tell me, traveler, where has the wind carried you on your grand adventure?

And who knows, maybe a chance encounter with a wandering bard like me will lead you to the treasures you seek, or at least, a good laugh and a melody to warm your heart.
